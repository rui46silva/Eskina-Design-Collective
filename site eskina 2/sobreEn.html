About us!

Eskina Design Collective , was born in the difficult times of the Covid-19 pandemic as a creative haven. Personality, creativity and communication are the watchwords for an always open collective that challenges the visual reality of everyday life. This is the designation for a creative escape from our daily lives, where we believe that when we don't know the direction to take, we can always find the best solution around ESKINA. We are Eskina design collective.
